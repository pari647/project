Diwali

Diwali, the "Festival of Lights," celebrates the victory of light over darkness and good over evil. It's marked by: * Lighting diyas (lamps) and candles: Symbolizing the dispelling of darkness. * Decorating homes: With rangoli and lights. * Worshipping Goddess Lakshmi: For wealth and prosperity. * Exchanging gifts and sweets: Strengthening bonds with family and friends. * Bursting fireworks: A joyous part of the celebration. * It is celebrated on the new moon night of the Hindu month Kartika. * Celebrated by Hindus, Jains, and Sikhs. .
