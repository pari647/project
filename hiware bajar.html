Hiware Bazar: A Model Village

Hiware Bazar is a small village in Maharashtra, India, that has become a model for sustainable development and community-led initiatives.

About Hiware Bazar:

  • Located in Ahmednagar district, Maharashtra
  • Transformed from a drought-prone village to a prosperous one
  • Known for its community-led initiatives in water conservation, agriculture, and education

Success Stories:

  • Water conservation efforts
  • Sustainable agriculture practices
  • Improved education and healthcare facilities
