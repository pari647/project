Shiv Jayanti

Shiv Jayanti is a festival celebrating the birth anniversary of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj, the great Maratha warrior king. It is celebrated with great enthusiasm in Maharashtra and other parts of India.

Shivaji Maharaj was known for his bravery, strategic brilliance, and administrative skills. He established a powerful Maratha kingdom and played a crucial role in resisting Mughal rule.

On this auspicious day, people remember his contributions to Indian history and culture. Celebrations often include processions, cultural programs, and historical reenactments.
