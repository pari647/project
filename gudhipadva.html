Gudi Padwa

Gudi Padwa marks the beginning of the Hindu New Year according to the lunisolar calendar. It is a significant festival celebrated in Maharashtra, Goa, and other regions.

The Gudi, a decorated bamboo staff, is a key symbol of the festival.

Traditional sweets like Puran Poli are prepared and shared.
